Solving the diff eq gives some of the [[Kinematic Equations]].
$$\begin{align}
\ddot{x}(t)&=\frac{F_{o}}{m} \\
\dot{x}(t)&=\int \ddot{x}(t)\,dt=v_{o}+\frac{F_{o}}{m}t \\
x(t)&=\int \dot{x}(t)\,dt=x_{o}+v_{o}t+\frac{F_{o}}{m}t^2
\end{align}
